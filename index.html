About

Hi, my name is Ramir. Since 2017, I have been working as a front-end developer. My technical skills are in HTML and CSS, and I have a little understanding of JavaScript because I'm more of a website builder guy, but I can still manage some coding and cross-platform testing. I'm also proficient in Photoshop and other picture editing tools. In addition, I operate as a freelance website developer, focusing on website builders like WordPress, ClickFunnels, GoHighLevel, and others.

What Sets Me Apart?

Website Builder Maestro

I specialize in harnessing the full potential of popular website builders to create visually appealing and responsive websites. Whether it's Wix, Squarespace, or WordPress, I transform ideas into reality with finesse.

Minimal Coding, Maximum Impact

Say goodbye to the intimidation of intricate code! I bring your visions to life by maximizing the capabilities of website builders, ensuring a polished and professional online presence without the need for extensive coding.

Design that Speaks Volumes

A great website is not just about functionality; it's also about aesthetics. I have a keen eye for design, ensuring that your website not only works flawlessly but also captivates your audience with its visual appeal.

Collaborative Approach

Your input matters! I work closely with clients to understand their unique needs and preferences, translating them into a digital masterpiece. Communication is key, and your satisfaction is my priority.
